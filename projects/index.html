Wasserstein GANs for Sparsely-sampled Ultrasound Image Reconstruction

Real-time on-device processing of high-resolution ultrasound images can be very resource intensive. What if we could combine efficient sensing ideas from compressive sensing together with efficient image reconstruction ideas from GANs? The result is a fast, efficient and effective way for ultrasound imaging on resource-limited imagers!

Loss-predictor Neural Nets for active data crowdsourcing
